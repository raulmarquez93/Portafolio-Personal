Projects

Moto Gear Hub

I developed an individual website as my final project for my degree, combining the sale of motorcycle accessories with a social network for motorcycle enthusiasts. I used Angular for the frontend, Laravel for the API, and MySQL with phpMyAdmin to manage the database. This project reinforced my skills in web development and the integration of dynamic functionalities. I faced challenges such as optimizing the user experience and implementing interactive features, tackling them proactively and with perseverance. Although it was an individual project, I took responsibility for each stage of the development, demonstrating commitment and a strong work ethic. I am proud to have created a platform that facilitates product purchases and fosters community among motorcyclists.
